About

SF Form 10, is a document used in the Philippine educational system to record and monitor the progress of students. This document provides information about a student's academic journey, including personal details, enrollment history, grades, and attendance.

Limitation

The eSchool form-10 System, like any software, may have some weaknesses such as: 1. Security Vulnerabilities: Any system connected to sensitive student data needs robust security measures to prevent unauthorized access or data breaches. 2. Dependency on Internet Connectivity: Being an online system, it relies heavily on internet connectivity. If the connection is poor or unreliable, it could hinder access and usage. 3. Technical Glitches: It could encounter technical issues like bugs, crashes, or downtime, disrupting its functionality.

Purpose

The eSchool form-10 system serves as a comprehensive platform for schools, teachers, and students to manage and streamline various administrative tasks related to student attendance, performance tracking, and reporting. For schools, it provides a centralized database for student records, simplifies attendance monitoring, and facilitates communication with parents. Teachers can efficiently record attendance, input grades, and access student information, while students can view their academic progress and attendance records, promoting accountability and transparency in the educational process.
